Chicken

A chicken (Gallus gallus domesticus) is a kind of domesticated bird. It is raised in many places for its meat and eggs. They are usually kept by humans as livestock. ... A male chicken is called a rooster or a cockerel

Beef

One of the main objectives of cattle rearing is to obtain meat or beef. Beef cattle are much more widely distributed than dairy cattle because they are harder and can tolerate wider range of physical and climatic conditions.

Sushi

Sushi (寿司, すし, 鮨, or 鮓) is a traditional food from Japan. The word "Sushi" comes from the Japanese word "Su" (酢) meaning vinegar, and "Meshi" (飯), meaning rice. ... The rice is mixed with vinegar, salt, and sugar. Sushi is known for having raw or cooked seafood in it, but it sometimes has non-fish foods such as vegetables
